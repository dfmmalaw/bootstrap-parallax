ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Dean Friedland and I am a developer. I have an incredible wife (Ashley), daughter (Reese), and son (Ryder), and there is nothing better than being a husband and father. I love all things martial arts and will train and compete in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu until the day I die! I also love to surf and have saltwater in my veins. I hope you enjoy my site and thank you for visiting!
