ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Dean Friedland and I am a developer. I have an incredible wife (Ashley), daughter (Reese), and son (Ryder), and there is nothing better than being a husband and father. I love all things martial arts and will train and compete in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu until the day I die! I also love to surf and have saltwater in my veins. I live in Kennesaw, Georgia and the closest beach is hours away, which I hate :)

I am originally from South Africa but have lived in the US since I was a kid, after a six year stint in Canada. I have also lived in both Ecuador and Costa Rica (both of which have killer waves) and am blessed to have traveled to many other countries in this amazing world. I hope you enjoy my site and thank you for visiting!
